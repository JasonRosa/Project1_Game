MALL OF DUTY

Zombie Apocalypse

INSTRUCTIONS

Zombies have taken over your local shopping mall and they are very hungry!

Avoid them and use the shopping cart to push them out of the way before they eat you!

Use the arrow keys to move left, right, up and down. You win 30 points

for successfully collecting the shopping cart. Win 80 points for each zombie

you succesfully kill using the cart. If you make it out alive you win!
